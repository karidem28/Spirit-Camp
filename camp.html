ABOUT CAMP

ORGANIZERS OF SPIRIT TIME

Spirit Time is a place of unity and service.We have been organizing winter and summer camps for years. In total, more than 1,500 unique participants visited our camps. # spiritcamp22 will be our fourth winter camp.

In short, our winter camps are: a lot of young people, a cozy atmosphere, a common desire for a deep knowledge of God. My team and I do our best to create the best possible atmosphere for you, so that you can relax spiritually and physically, grow spiritually and find many new acquaintances. Our program is very busy, but you will have a lot of free time.

CHEVALIER PANORAMA BASE

Chevalier Panorama is located in the village of Yablunytsia, at an altitude of about 1000 m above sea level. Thanks to this, from the windows of the hotel you can watch the highest points of Ukraine - Hoverla and the peaks of the Montenegrin ridge.
